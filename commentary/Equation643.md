## A right-unit fourth-powers law

This law states that the powers `y◇((y◇y)◇y)` are right units.

The analogues for squares, left cubes and right cubes are [law 11](https://teorth.github.io/equational_theories/implications/?11), [law 56](https://teorth.github.io/equational_theories/implications/?56) and [law 108](https://teorth.github.io/equational_theories/implications/?108), and for different fourth powers are [law 440](https://teorth.github.io/equational_theories/implications/?440), law 643, [law 846](https://teorth.github.io/equational_theories/implications/?846), [law 1049](https://teorth.github.io/equational_theories/implications/?1049), and [law 1252](https://teorth.github.io/equational_theories/implications/?1252).
